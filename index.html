My Journey to Sundsgården and Copenhagen

The Night Train

The day that would begin the next chapter of my journey here in Sweden was finally here and I was both filled with all types of emotions.
Relief that this day was here, nervous due to the challenge ahead but first and foremost excitement, excitement for what may lay ahead.

I have often admired the Swedish transport services as the night train has continually enabled me to tuck in nicely for a good sleep whilst
traversing the length or breadth of the country, to wake up in my next desired location. It's always been on time and a good nights sleep.
UNTIL NOW

As pointed out by many friends and accomplices that i've met here in Sweden, sometimes, their services don't operate in the way you
would like them to. Unfortunately for me their was a fault with the train which would cause loud screeching sound every time it slowed
or navigated a slight bend

This led to a very disrupted sleep mainly spending the evening staring at the ceiling and in turn, making me sleep through my stop in
Helsingborg stop and finding myself in Lund city. After a few more train cancellations and a bus that did't arrive, I found my way to Sundsgården Folkhögskola!
